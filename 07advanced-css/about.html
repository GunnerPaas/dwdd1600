About Gunner

Gunner is a 21 year old student currently studying Graphic Design at Utah Valley University. He is currently living in the Provo, Utah area, and alongside attending school he is working full time at Ken Garff Ford in American Fork, Utah. While growing up in Utah he fell in love with the beautiful landscapes in the area and has been taking pictures from a young age. After graduating from Lehi High School in 2021, Gunner took a 2 year hiatus on his photography to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. After returning home in August of 2023 he has since been able to resume his work.
